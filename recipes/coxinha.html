Coxinha

Description

Typical brazilian chicken snack.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 4 cups of water
  • 3 envelopes of chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon of margarine
  • Milk to bersmich
  • Breadcrumbs with powdered chicken seasoning to bersmich
  • Oil to fry it

Filling

  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1 clove of garlic squeezed
  • 1 coocked and shredded chicken breast
  • 1 powdered chicken seasoning sachet
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 chopped tomato
  • Salt
  • Chopped parsley for sprinkling
  • Chopped chive for sprinkling

Steps

Dough

  1. Boil the water with the chicken broth and the margarine
  2. Add all of the flour at once, mixing it until it unglues itself from the pan
  3. Let it cool down

Filling

  1. Heat the oil and saut√© the onion and garlic
  2. Add the chicken and remaining filling ingredients
  3. Let it boil until thickened
  4. Sprinkle the parsley and chive

Assemble

  1. With your hands greased in oil, grab little portions of the dough, add the filling and mold the coxinhas into a rounded triangle shape
  2. Bermish it first on the milk, then on the breadcrumbs
  3. Deep fry it, few at a time, until it golden
  4. Serve
