Pão de Queijo (Cheese Bread)

Ingredients

  • 500g of sweet powder (polvilho doce)
  • 2 eggs
  • 250ml milk
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 packet of grated parmesan cheese
  • 350g of grated muzzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Put the milk on to boil
  2. On bowl, put the sweet powder and add the salt

    3. Mix it well then, still hot, add the boiled milk.

    Mix it again, first with a spoon then with your hands.

  3. Add the parmesan cheese and half the muzzarella cheese
  4. Add 1 egg
  5. Add the rest of the muzzarella cheese

    6. The dough should come off the pan and your hand easily.

    If it is too dry, add another egg, if it is too oily, add more sweet powder.

  6. Separate the dough into small balls
  7. Put it in the baking tray leaving small spaces between each unit
  8. Bake at 446°F (230°C) until golden
  9. Wait for it to cool it then serve
